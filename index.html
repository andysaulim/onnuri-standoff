The ONNURI Standoff

In late 2025, South Korea dispatched the research vessel ONNURI to inspect Chinese structures built inside a jointly-managed maritime zone — structures Beijing never sought permission to build.

China's coast guard blocked the mission, deploying rubber boats within 1km of the target platform. This animation traces the four-day confrontation using live AIS vessel tracking data.
